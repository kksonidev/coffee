A Day in Your Life

Every day is different, but here's what a typical shift might look like

7:00 AM

Opening Prep

Grind fresh beans, set up espresso machines, stock pastries. Morning playlist sets the vibe.

8:00 AM

Morning Rush

The café fills up! You're steaming milk, pulling shots, greeting regulars by name, and keeping the energy high.

10:30 AM

Mid-Morning Break

30-minute paid break. Enjoy a free drink and maybe try that new pastry recipe.

11:00 AM

Creative Time

Practice latte art, help train a new barista, or experiment with a new seasonal drink idea.

12:00 PM

Lunch Service

Different crowd, different drinks. More cold brews and iced lattes as the day warms up.

3:00 PM

Afternoon Shift Change

Hand off to evening crew or stay for closing. Clean equipment, restock supplies, prep for tomorrow.
